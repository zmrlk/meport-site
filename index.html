How it works

Three steps. Five minutes.

01

Drop your files

CV, notes, bookmarks, chat exports — anything you have about yourself. Meport reads them on your machine and builds your profile. Nothing is uploaded.

02

Answer a few questions

Your files fill most of it automatically. Meport only asks about what's missing — a few quick questions, not a long survey.

03

Export everywhere

Pick the AI tools you use. Meport sends your profile in the right format for each one. ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, Copilot — all supported.
