Privacy

Your data stays yours.

No servers. No accounts. No tracking. Here's exactly what happens with your data.

100% local processing

Meport runs entirely on your machine. Your profile, your files, your answers — everything stays in a local JSON file. We don't have servers to send data to.

What we read

When you drop files (CV, notes, bookmarks), Meport reads them locally to extract profile dimensions. It looks for: occupation, skills, interests, communication patterns. Files are never uploaded anywhere.

Privacy filter

Before any AI processing, a built-in filter blocks sensitive patterns: passwords, financial data, medical records, legal documents, identity numbers. This runs automatically — you can't disable it.

When AI is used

AI is optional — only used if you choose AI interview mode or Layer 3 insights. When used, filtered profile data goes directly to YOUR chosen provider (Claude, OpenAI, or Ollama for fully offline). Meport is never a middleman.

What exports contain

Exports are plain text rules like "Max 5 lines for simple questions" or "Skip praise." They contain behavioral preferences, not personal data. No names, addresses, or sensitive info goes into exports.

Fully offline option

Use Ollama as your AI provider and nothing ever leaves your machine. Zero network requests. Zero telemetry. The entire profiling pipeline runs without internet.
